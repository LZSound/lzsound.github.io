About

My musical journey began at an early age with guitar and piano, which quickly became my main instruments and form of expression. I have continued playing ever since, developing my style as a self-taught composer through research, listening, and experimentation.

Music production started as creative exploration and evolved into a true artistic necessity. I compose cinematic music driven by emotion and atmosphere, transforming ideas into soundtracks designed for films, trailers, and narrative worlds.
