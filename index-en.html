About

My musical journey began at an early age with the study of guitar, followed shortly by piano, which soon became my primary instrument and main form of expression. Since then, I have never stopped playing, continuing in recent years as a self-taught composer, refining my style through research, active listening, and experimentation.

Music production started as a creative exploration and gradually evolved into a true expressive necessity. I compose cinematic music guided by emotion and atmosphere, transforming ideas and sensations into soundtracks designed to accompany images, stories, and narrative worlds.
